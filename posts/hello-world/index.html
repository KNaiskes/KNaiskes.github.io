Hello World

Posted on 16 December, 2020

I was thinking of starting a blog for weeks but never really found the time to start developing it. And I am saying developing because like most developers I know, I wanted my blog to be made from scratch by me using a static site generator. You know the reason… just for fun!

So, the static site generator I am using is Hugo with a very simple - minimalistic theme, I made in just a few hours (yes it took me so long for such a simple theme).

The best part is that Hugo as most of all the other static site generators, supports Markdown for writing posts. This means that as an Emacs user, I can write my posts in Org Mode and then export them to Markdown. Perhaps one day I will write about Org Mode and you may understand my excitement!

Bye!
